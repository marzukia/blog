None of this was a plan. I've spent years building little tools whenever something annoyed me, or some website wanted a subscription to do one obvious thing. They just piled up on my drive doing nothing. So I'm finally dumping them online, for free. There's a charting library in here too, but mostly it's browser tools. No accounts, no uploads, no paywalls.
